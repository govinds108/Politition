About Us

Welcome to Polititian, your #1 platform for creating petitions. Founded in 2019 we are group of high school students who believe everyone should have a voice. Our mission is to aid people who wish to express their thoughts and provide a channel where voices may be heard. We hope you enjoy our site, if you have any questions please feel free to contact us.
